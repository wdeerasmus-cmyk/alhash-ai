How It Works

From Data to Decision in Milliseconds

Our four-stage pipeline transforms raw market signals into actionable intelligence with full auditability.

1
Ingest & Normalise
We aggregate 500+ alternative and traditional data feeds, normalising them into a unified time-series store.
2
Model & Score
Ensemble models score every asset in our universe every 15 seconds, ranking by expected risk-adjusted return.
3
Optimise Portfolio
A convex optimiser constructs the target portfolio subject to your constraints—sector limits, ESG screens, drawdown caps.
4
Execute & Monitor
Smart order routing executes rebalances with minimal market impact while continuous monitoring guards against drift.
